Adam Levine Announces the Sex of His and Behati Prinsloo's Second Child

Behati Prinsloo and Adam Levine are having a baby girl. The singer made the announcement on The Ellen DeGeneres Show today, Us Weekly reports.

"It is a girl. We are having another girl," Levine said on air. This marks his and the Victoria's Secret Angel's second child—and second daughter—together. Their first, Dusty Rose, was born in September 2016.

Prinsloo announced her second pregnancy two months ago with a bikini-clad Instagram fit for a VS model. "Round 2....." she wrote in her caption.

Not long after that, she proudly flaunted her baby bump in #OOTD mirror selfies...

...And on the red carpet.

Prinsloo is nearly seven months along, her husband revealed on Ellen, which means the pair may be welcoming a new member of the family at the beginning of the new year. "She’s awesome. She’s like a champion of the world. She’s killing it," he said. "You would never know she’s almost seven months pregnant. God bless her."

The Angel, 28, and Maroon 5 frontman, 38, started dating in 2012 and tied the knot in 2014. According to the singer, the couple may be welcoming many more children together in the future. When asked if he wants more kids, Levine said, "Probably. I want a lot. I thrive with chaos. I really, generally enjoy and love it."

The same apparently goes for Prinsloo. "She was an only child. So she wants like a hundred babies. But I don’t know if I could do that," Levine joked.

Congratulations to the happy couple!
