Ryan Kennedy Bio (Wiki)

Who is Ryan Kennedy?

Ryan Kennedy is a Canadian actor and model. He is popular for his amazing acting in The Invisible (2007), Hannah’s Law (2012), and Caprica (2009).

Recently, he played the character of Gary for two episodes in Them, TV series. His upcoming project titled Love and Communication as Rob is under post-production.

Ryan Kennedy- Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

His birthdate is 06 Dec 1982 and he was born in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada. The details of his mother along with his father are not disclosed.

Ryan Kennedy’s ancestry is Caucasian and has two younger brothers, Adam Kennedy plus Micah Kennedy.

Regarding his academics, Ryan Kennedy attended Springs Christian Academy.

Ryan Kennedy- Professional Life, Career

Ryan Kennedy is a popular face of the entertainment world. His first movie was named Jack of 1996 where he played the role of George.

He also other movies titled While I Was Gone in 2004, The Invisible in 2006, Poison Ivy: The Secret Society in 2008, The Break-Up Artist in 2009, Lucky in Love in 2014, Marrying Mr. Darcy in 2018, and more.

In addition, Ryan Kennedy appeared in Whistler (2007) as Travis Hollier, Caprica (2010) as Odin Sinclair, Hellcats (2010-11) as Jake Harrow, Major Crimes (2014-17) as Ricky Raydor, and Tin Star (2017-19) as Constable Nick McGillen.

The list of his other notable TV projects are,
