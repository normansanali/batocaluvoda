Thomas Announces His Engagement to Paris

In the latest The Bold and the Beautiful preview for June 24 – 28, how does Poppy know Tom? Read on and watch the preview below.

Bill has been on cloud nine playing house with his former flame Poppy and his supposed daughter Luna. We’re still not sold on that DNA test Poppy’s sister Li did, which confirmed that Bill was the father. Last week at Il Giardino, Poppy and Tom spotted one another, and it was clear they had history!

Coming up Poppy answers the door to Bill’s house, well now her and Luna’s house too, and finds Tom standing there with a pizza from Il Giardano! She is shunned and asks, “What are you doing here? Could Tom actually be Luna’s father?

After Hope admitted to her mother that she had feelings for Finn, Brooke told her to make them go away as he was Steffy’s husband! Hope assured her mom she would deal with her feelings and overcome them. Of course, that’s when her ex Thomas returned frm Paris with Douglas, but his son isn’t the only one who also came home!

Next week, Thomas tells his father, “I’m glad I finally found someone who wants the same things as me.”

A stunned Hope asks, “What is going on here?” Thomas stands by Paris and Douglas, and informs Hope, “Paris and I are engaged.” Hope looks at the ring on her finger. Thomas adds, “We’re going to be married.”
