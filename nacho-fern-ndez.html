Career

After making his first-team debut in 2011, Nacho has spent his whole career at Real Madrid, making over 200 appearances and winning numerous titles, including four Champions Leagues. He first represented Spain at the senior level in 2013, and he was a member of the squad at the 2018 FIFA World Cup.

Nacho made his international debut in the 2–2 draw against Chile in Geneva, replacing Real Madrid teammate Sergio Ramos early in the second half. Nacho has been nominated to Spain’s final FIFA World Cup squad. On June 15, he scored his first goal for his country, starting at right-back in a 3–3 tie against Portugal in the group stage.
