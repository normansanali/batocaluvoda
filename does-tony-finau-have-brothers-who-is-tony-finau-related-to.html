Does Tony Finau have brothers? Who is Tony Finau related to?

Tony Finau is an American professional golfer who currently plays on the PGA Tour.

Does Tony Finau have brothers? Who is Tony Finau related to?

The golfer comes from a large family and has also made a large family on his own with his wife, Alayna.

Finau was born to Kelepi “Gary” Finau and Ravena Finau. His mother sadly passed away in a car accident in 2011.

Finau grew up with six siblings; four brothers and two sisters. His brother Gipper, at the age of 16, made the cut in the Utah EnergySolutions Championship. He did not succeed as a tournament professional.

The love for sports does not end in his family, his extended family also shares a love for sports. Finau is the cousin of NBA basketball player Jabari Parker as well as the cousin of former NFL football player Haloti Ngata.
