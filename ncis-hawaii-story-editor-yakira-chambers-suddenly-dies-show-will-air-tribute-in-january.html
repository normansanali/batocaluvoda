NCIS Hawaii Story Editor Yakira Chambers Suddenly Dies; Show Will Air Tribute in January

NCIS Hawai’i has lost a team member.

Yakira Chambers, a story writer on the popular CBS series, has sadly died at the young age of 42.

Click inside to read more…

Yakira‘s family shared the news with Deadline, who reports that she passed from acute asphyxia. She was with her mother outside a mall in Newport Beach, Calif. on Wednesday last week, when she began suffering breathing difficulties and then collapsed.

For her role on NCIS: Hawai’i, Yakira joined the show as a staff writer in the debut season, and was then promoted to story editor for season two.

She wrote four episodes, and was part of ViacomCBS’s Writers Mentoring Program in 2020-2021.

NCIS: Hawai’i will be airing a tribute to her in January, just a week after a three-hour NCIS universe crossover event.

In addition to being a story editor on the show, Yakira also starred in HBO’s Insecure; Saban Films’ John Henry, the comedy short series The Misadventures of Awkward Black Girl; and the comedy series M.O. Diaries.
