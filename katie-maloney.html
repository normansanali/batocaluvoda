Katie Maloney News - Us Weekly

Name:Katie Maloney

Hometown:Park City, Utah

Relationship:Single

Birthday:January 16, 1987

About

Born in Utah, Katie Maloney moved to California to explore an acting career. She appeared in TV shows such as Harper’s Globe, 2009: A True Story and BlackBoxTV. Maloney rose to stardom after she joined the cast of Bravo’s Vanderpump Rules in 2013.

The reality series explored Maloney’s ups and downs as she balanced a waitress job at SUR, her beauty, fashion and lifestyle website Pucker and Pout and her relationship with Tom Schwartz. As the series continued, Maloney moved on from the restaurant and started her own podcast, “You’re Gonna Love Me.”

After five years of dating, Maloney and Schwartz tied the knot on screen in 2016. They remarried three years later in a Las Vegas ceremony following past paperwork issues. In March 2022, the pair announced their decision to call it quits after more than a decade together. Their divorce was finalized later that year.

Maloney has since been linked to Satchel Clendenin.
