Street Fighter X Tekken Roster Finalized, PC Version Dated

Recommended Videos

With only a few weeks away from its March 6 release, gamers worldwide are begging for the final few spots open in Street Fighter X Tekken to be revealed. Perhaps as a sort of Valentines Day present, Capcom has not only granted that request, but revealed the date for the PC version of the game to hit shelves.

First, the release date. Gamers who want to bring the crossover fighter to their PCs can expect the game to be available on retail and virtual shelves on May 11. Now that that’s out of the way…

Joining the roster on the Street Fighter side of things are M. Bison and Akuma, two fighters which really don’t come as any surprise given their popularity. The Tekken roster gets a boost from Jin and Ogre.

So for those keeping track, the full roster now looks like this:

That brings the roster to an impressive 38 fighters, or 43 if you’re playing on a Sony device.

For you, the day M. Bison graced Street Fighter X Tekken was the greatest day of your life. But for me, it was Tuesday.
