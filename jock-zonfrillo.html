Jock had a younger sister, and his zodiac sign was Leo. He adhered to Christianity as his religion. He received his education at Belmont Academy in Ayr.

Death

Jock Zonfrillo died on April 30, 2023, in Melbourne, Australia, at the age of 46. When police were called to Zagame’s House hotel to conduct a welfare check, they discovered his body.
