Blake Lively Leaves Thirsty Comment on Ryan Reynolds's Instagram

  • Blake Lively gushed over husband Ryan Reynolds in a comment on the actor's Instagram last night.
  • After the Deadpool star posted a photo shoot to promote his Aviation Gin brand, Lively wrote that she's "feeling very happy about my life choices right now."
  • Lively and Reynolds are currently expecting their third child.

Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds are notorious for trolling each other on Instagram, but last night, the former Gossip Girl star switched things up and shamelessly showed her husband some love for all of their followers to see.

Reynolds kicked things off with a series of images from a photoshoot promoting his gin brand, Aviation. He smolders in a houndstooth jacket, poses dapperly in a double-breasted suit, and looks handsomely quirky in a fox-print tie. You can't blame Lively gushing over her husband in this suave slideshow.

The actress commented on Reynolds's post, "Feeling very happy about my life choices right about now..." She and Reynolds tied the knot in 2012, welcomed two daughters (James, 4, and Ines, 2), and have a third child on the way. They announced that they're expecting in May during the red carpet premiere of Detective Pikachu.

The Deadpool actor recently paid homage to Lively after she gave him a precious gift last month: a painting of Reynolds as a paper boy (his first job) in front of his childhood home. "This piece of art is the greatest present my wife has ever given me," he said of the piece, crafted by artist Danny Galieote. "If there’s ever a fire, I’m grabbing this first. I’ll come back for Blake," he joked.

Banter aside, Reynolds paid an honest tribute to his wife when he was honored with a Walk of Fame Star in 2016. "You make everything better, absolutely everything in my life better," he told Lively at the ceremony. "You’ve made me the father of my dreams when I thought I only had fun uncle potential."
