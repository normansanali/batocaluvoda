Christos Kyrgios: Who is Nick Kyrgios brother?

Christos Kyrgios is a trainer and fitness coach. He works as a fitness coach for Ally’s Angels & Alphas, a business that specializes in training for WBFF pro competitions.

Christos completed his study at the university level and earned degrees in law and commerce. Christos entered the business world after graduating from college to begin his early career.

Earlier in his career, he also served as an investment advisor and a legal policy advisor for Parliament.

Christos Kyrgios has achieved renown on his own, but he also benefits from the notoriety of his younger brother, Australian tennis sensation Nick Kyrgios.

Alicia Gowans is Christos’ fiancée, and they are already engaged, unlike Nick. Christos and Alicia are both employed in the fitness sector. For the past fifteen years, Alicia has worked in the fitness industry.
