Sex Diaries Is Going to Be a TV Show Now

“Sex Diaries” has hit the big time! The popular, controversial Daily Intel feature, which asks sexually active folks across the country to chronicle a week of sex-having (or a week frustratedly fantasizing about it), will be adapted for television, Vulture hears. The “Sex Diaries” show will be set in New York City and follow ten separate but interconnected characters, and fittingly, multiple partners are involved in shepherding the project to the small screen: Writer Tom Spezialy (Desperate Housewives, Reaper) will script the pilot for ABC Studios and producers Mark Gordon and Nicholas Pepper. Finally, some representation for pansexual grade-school teachers visiting swingers parties in Williamsburg! Cross your fingers for this one. Cross everything.
