AEW Continental Crown Title Match Set For 1/6 AEW Collision

A big title match has been announced for this week’s AEW Collision.

During the January 3 episode of AEW Dynamite, Brian Cage, Bryan Keith, Trent Beretta, and El Hijo del Vikingo all clashed in a four-way eliminator match for a shot at Eddie Kingston’s AEW Continental Crown Championship.

At one point, Danhausen entered the ring and “cursed” Cage before Beretta took control. Ultimately, Beretta pinned Keith to earn the title match against Eddie Kingston this Saturday in Charlotte.

Below is the updated lineup for January 6:

  • : Eddie Kingston (c) vs. Trent Beretta
  • FTR (Dax Harwood & Cash Wheeler) vs. House of Black (Malakai Black & Buddy Matthews)

WrestleZone will have complete coverage of Collision as it airs this Saturday.
