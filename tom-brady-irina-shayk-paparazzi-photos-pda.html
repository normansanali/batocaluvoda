So, Tom Brady Was Photographed Caressing Irina Shayk's Face in His Car

Are Tom Brady and Irina Shayk Hollywood’s next big It couple?

New paparazzi photos published by Page Six show the duo getting cozy in Los Angeles this past weekend. The outlet reported that Brady picked up Shayk from the Hotel Bel-Air on Friday afternoon and drove them to his L.A. house, where they reportedly stayed until the following morning. Pictures show the rumored new couple smiling widely at each other, with Brady even caressing Shayk’s face at one point during the drive.

The supermodel looked casually stylish in a white strappy minidress paired with a black sweater tied around her waist, plus gold hoop earrings, black shield sunglasses, a black shoulder bag, black Adidas sneakers, a cross pendant necklace, and a silver wristwatch. Meanwhile, the NFL player wore a light green long-sleeved shirt with a pair of light-wash jeans.

The photos emerged after Shayk’s rep denied reports that she had flirted with the athlete at a mutual friend’s Italian wedding last month. At the time, a source told Page Six that Shayk had made “a beeline” for the football star and stayed close to him throughout the weekend. Her manager responded with a statement to the Daily Mail, saying, “This story is completely false. It is a totally malicious and fictional account of the evening.”

Tom Brady and Gisele Bündchen

In October 2022, Brady and Gisele Bündchen announced their plans to divorce after 13 years of marriage. The former couple share two children together, 13-year-old Benjamin and 10-year-old Vivian. (Brady also shares 15-year-old son Jack with ex Bridget Moynahan.)

Shayk was previously attached to Bradley Cooper, with whom she shares six-year-old daughter Lea De Seine. The two officially split in 2019 after four years of dating.
