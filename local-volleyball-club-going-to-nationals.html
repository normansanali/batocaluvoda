Local volleyball club going to nationals

The Ridgetop Volleyball Club said two of its teams qualified for a national tournament coming up.

They said a 16U and 17U team are leaving Sunday for a four-day tournament in Florida starting Monday, June 26 with championship games being played Thursday, June 29.

Coaches and players said the tournament will be a grind with at least three games to play each day.

The club said a 14U team represented them at nationals last week.
