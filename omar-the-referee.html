All About TikTok Sensation Omar the Referee

Omar Wilson, aka Omar the Referee (age: 47 years; born in 1976), is an American basketball referee and coach based in Baltimore, Maryland. People know him as Omar the Referee, his internet name, which got popular after one of his TIkTok videos went viral.

We are talking about a small clip where Omar could be seen training young school kids. People made this video a meme material and made their version which made Omar famous worldwide.

In August 2023, news came that Omar, The Referee, committed suicide due to cyberbullying. Netizens quickly believed the word and expressed their condolences to the grieved family.

After some days, someone claimed that Wilson was not dead, and his death rumors were fake. Soon debate among netizens regarding Omar started, which gave birth to many rumors.

Omar the Referee’s Death Rumors 

People on the internet debate this topic, and everybody has their version of the story regarding the viral referee. Some trusted platforms claim that BBC first revealed Omar’s death.

Upon checking BBC’s news website, we did not find any article about the death of a Baltimore resident. Soon we saw a screenshot of a tweet from BBC claiming the 47-year-old referee died.

Looking at the screenshot, it appears that somebody edited the photo to make the news of Wilson’s death accurate; also, we did not find any such tweet on BBC’s Twitter handle.

Omar the Referee Declared Alive

On the one hand, we have untrustworthy sources of information that claim that Omar is dead; on the other hand, we have a TikTok handle claiming the referee is alive.

The TikTok handle with username ‘@omarwilson460’ appears to be the authentic account of Omar. According to this account, the beloved basketball coach is alive and living a sound life in his hometown Baltimore. Our take is that he is alive, and people spread fake rumors about his death.
