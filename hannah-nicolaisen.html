American Idol Star Hannah Nicolaisen Wiki | Bio

Hannah Nicolaisen (age: 23 years; born on December 28, 1999) is a singer from Houston, Texas, who became a star after competing in season 21 of the popular music reality show “American Idol.” When this young singer with Danish roots auditioned for the ABC show, it had been only a couple of months since she started exploring the art of singing.

To earn her Golden Ticket to Hollywood Week, Hannah sang Bob Dylan’s “Make You Feel My Love.” Her singing made the “American Idol” judges clap for her. Lionel Richie pointed out Nicolaisen that she has a unique voice that people pray to have; Luke Bryan also praised Hannah’s voice and told her that she was born to become a singer.

Ultimately, all the judges said “Yes” to her and handed her the Golden Ticket, which allowed her to move to Hollywood Week, which was the next step.

Hannah Nicolaisen In The Top 26

After working her magic at the audition round, Hannah moved to the next step, which was Hollywood Week, but this rookie singer shocked the judges and audience with her original song. Her performance was so mesmerizing that everybody listening was forced to clap.

Next came the Showstopper Round, where the best 55 contestants performed to earn a place in the top 26.

Nicolaisen was a bit nervous this time because she knew about the cutthroat competition, so she decided to spice up things and chose Gavin DeGraw’s song “I Don’t Want to Be” and gave a power-packed performance which helped her land a place in the top 26.

How Old is Hannah Nicolaisen?

On December 28, 1999, Hannah Nicolaisen (age 23) was born in Houston, Texas, America. As per sources, her mother, Carol TeWell Nicolaisen, is a homemaker, and her father is a businessman.

Hannah spent 18 years of her life in Houston; she studied at Cypress Woods High School, where she blossomed into a fantastic athlete. She was on the women’s Volleyball team and played numerous matches.

In 2017 she completed high school and moved to Arkansas City, Kansas, to study Science at Cowley College. Two years later, she moved to the University of Illinois Springfield, where she earned her Bachelor’s degree in Exercise and Psychology in 2022.

What did Hannah Nicolaisen Do Before Coming to American Idol?

Hannah was 19 when she began working; her first job was as Assistant Volleyball Coach at One Volleyball Academy. Meanwhile, she was a student-athlete representing Cowley College as a Volleyball player.

While attending the University of Illinois Springfield, she did an internship at Amazon as a Workplace Health and Safety Specialist; it was a 3 months long internship that gave the young girl the experience of working in a natural work environment.

In July 2022, after her Bachelor’s degree, Nicolaisen started working full-time as Technical Recruiter at Stratice LLC.
