Mexican Legend, Juan Gabriel, Dies At 66

published on September 21, 2024
Juan Gabriel, who was a Mexican singer and songwriter, died at the age of 66 at his home in Santa Monica, California. He died from a heart attack Sunday morning at 11:30. “Juan Gabriel” was actually a stage name, with his legal name being Alberto Aguilera Valadez. Fans also called him the “Divo de Juárez,” because of his flamboyant style. People all across Latin America, the Caribbean, and Spain listened to his romantic ballads and Mariachi music.
