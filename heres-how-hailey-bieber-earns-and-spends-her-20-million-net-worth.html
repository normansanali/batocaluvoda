Heres How Hailey Bieber Earns And Spends Her $20 Million Net Worth

Model Hailey Bieber rose to fame in the mid-2010s — and ever since she's been one of the most successful stars in the fashion industry. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Hailey is currently estimated to have a net worth of $20 million, and today we're taking a look at just how the star earns and spends all that money.

From making money through her very own YouTube channel to splurging on her super expensive skincare products — keep on scrolling to find out more about the model's wealth!

10 Earns: Modeling

Let's kick the list off with the most obvious way that Hailey Bieber earns her millions — through modeling. Hailey entered the modeling industry back in 2014 and ever since she's been one of the big names in the fashion industry. Alongside her besties such as Kendall Jenner and the Hadid sisters, Hailey has taken over the industry by storm. Over the years the model has worked with plenty of famous brands such as Guess, Ralph Lauren, and Tommy Hilfiger.

9 Spends: Luxurious Properties

Just like most rich and famous — one thing that Hailey Bieber tends to spend quite a lot of money on are properties. Last year she and her husband, Canadian musician Justin Bieber bought a $25.8 million mansion in the mountains above Beverly Hills within the illustrious Beverly Park. This surely won't be the last house that the couple decides to purchase as celebs tend to switch their properties rather quickly.

8 Earns: Youtube Channel

A recent source of income for Hailey Bieber is surely her YouTube channel which the model launched in March 2021.

So far, Hailey's channel features plenty of lifestyle, beauty, and fashion videos — and it gives Hailey's fans a glimpse into what the star is actually like behind the scenes. Currently, Hailey Bieber's YouTube channel has 1.13 million subscribers — but the number is bound to grow even more in the future.

7 Spends: Traveling

While traveling is definitely a big part of Hailey Bieber's work as she tends to model all across the globe — those who follow the star on social media definitely know that Hailey also loves traveling for her own soul. Together with her husband Justin Bieber, Hailey often goes on romantic getaways and while the two certainly seem down to earth, there's no doubt that their vacations cost a lot!

6 Earns: Starring In Music Videos

Because Hailey Bieber is an ultra-successful model, it's definitely not surprising that she has a couple of popular music videos under her belt. Over the course of her career, Hailey could be seen in quite a few music videos and her most notable ones are "On My Mind" by Cody Simpson, "Love to Love You Baby" by Baptiste Giabiconi, "10,000 Hours" by Dan + Shay and Justin Bieber, "Stuck with U" by Ariana Grande and Justin Bieber, and "PopStar" by DJ Khaled, Drake, and Justin Bieber.

5 Spends: Expensive Skincare

Of course, because Hailey Bieber is such a successful model it's very important for her to maintain healthy and good-looking skin. It's safe to say that Hailey is totally okay with spending quite a lot of money on skincare products and some products like the Furtuna Skin Due Alberi Biphase Moisturizing Oil cost up to $128. In total, her skincare products certainly add up to a couple of hundred dollars — but the model can definitely afford splurging on skincare.

4 Earns: Hosting

Another very common source of income for Hailey Bieber is doing work as a hostess. In 2015, Hailey was the host in a segment of the 2015 MTV Europe Music Awards.

In 2017, she began hosting the TBS show Drop the Mic together with Method Man. While Hailey Bieber certainly looks stunning — she has also proven that she can definitely host shows and we bet she will do more of that in the future!

3 Spends: Partying

Currently, Hailey Bieber is 24 years old and while she is happily married — one thing that she and her husband both still love doing is partying. As we all know partying — especially in Hollywood — can quickly cost a lot but for Hailey that is certainly not a problem. Every now and then the model certainly loves splurging on a fun night out and she usually documents it on her social media.

2 Earns: Product Collaborations

Of course, being a famous person often means that one gets plenty of opportunities for brand deals and Hailey has certainly done quite a few throughout her career. In 2016 she collaborated with the clothing brand The Daily Edited as well as the footwear brand Public Desire. Hailey has also dabbled in the makeup industry with the Australian brand ModelCo.

1 Spends: Her Dog

And finally, wrapping the list up is the fact that loves spoiling her and Justin's dog Oscar which the couple adopted in December 2018. As any pet parents out there know, having an animal in your household is certainly not cheap but it's safe to say that little Oscar is one thing that Hailey absolutely loves spending her money on!
