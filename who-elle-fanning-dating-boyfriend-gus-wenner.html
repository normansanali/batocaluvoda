Who Is Elle Fanning Dating? Star Steps Out With Rolling Stone CEO Gus Wenner

Elle Fanning has a new man in her life.

The actor, 25, was spotted walking hand in hand with Rolling Stone magazine CEO Gus Wenner, 33, in New York City, over the weekend.

Earlier in the evening, the two ate dinner with singer Lorde, actor Annabelle Dexter-Jones, and Gus’s brother visual artist Theo Wenner at West Village hot spot the Waverly Inn. They later went out for drinks at Corner Bistro.

Fanning wore a long black coat, loose wide-leg blue jeans, and glossy black pointed-toe flats. She carried a small black leather handbag, wore minimal makeup, and had her hair straight down. Wenner wore a gray flannel, straight-leg blue jeans, a boxy black jacket, and black boots. He accessorized with a tan scarf.

TheImageDirect.com

Their unexpected couple’s debut comes seven months after Fanning ended her five-year relationship with fellow actor Max Minghella. “But I’m a hopeless romantic. I believe in love at first sight. Call me crazy, but I believe in those things. I feel that it’s my destiny,” she told Harper’s Bazaar U.K. in April.

The actor did an interview with Rolling Stone in 2018, but it is unclear how she connected with and began dating Wenner. The businessman was previously linked to singer and actor Maya Hawke, in 2019, and model Jessica Gomes, in 2018.

Fanning lives in New York’s SoHo and is preparing for her Broadway debut. The play, a dark comedy family drama called Appropriate, opens next month and also stars Sarah Paulson.
