Why Did Sandra Bullock And Jesse James Divorce?

Jesse James admitted to cheating on Sandra Bullock, which was one of the primary reasons the Hollywood actress wanted to end things with her husband for good.

In an interview with Daily Mail, the 53-year-old said, "Yeah, I [Jesse James] did cheat on my wife [Sandra Bullock], yeah, I stood up and took accountability for it and apologized. And that's the end of the story." James further adds, "In general, both women and men cheat [on their partners]. It's part of life."

When Did Sandra Bullock Find Out About Jesse James' Cheating Incident?

The exact date that Sandra Bullock discovered Jesse James' cheating incident is unclear. After her messy divorce with her ex-husband, Sandra Bullock took a break from dating men for a period. However, after five years, she met her current partner Bryan Randall who co-parents Sandra's two adopted children, Louis Bardo and Laila. Now in their seventh year, Sandra Bullock and her 38-year-old partner still seem not to be fans of publicizing their relationship. What Happened To Sandra Bullock's Ex-Husband Jesse James After The Divorce?

After Jesse James admitted to cheating on his ex-wife, he continued dating several women until he and his current partner Alexis Dejoria decided to settle things between them.

I hope one day they can find it in their hearts to forgive me."

Does Sandra Bullock Have A Relationship With Jesse James' Children?

However, even before she decided to adopt her two children, she had already experienced a glimpse of motherhood as she became the stepmother of Jesse James' three children for five years.
