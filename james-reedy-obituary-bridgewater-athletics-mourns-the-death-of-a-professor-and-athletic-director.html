James Reedy Obituary, Bridgewater Athletics Mourns The Death Of A Professor And Athletic Director

James Reedy Obituary, Death – Bridgewater Athletics is profoundly saddened to learn of the passing of one of its most esteemed alumni, Dr. James “Jim” Reedy ’61. His legacy within the Bridgewater College community is nothing short of remarkable, as he wore many hats during his time at BC, leaving an indelible mark in academics and athletics.

Dr. Jim Reedy was a true embodiment of the Bridgewater spirit. As a student-athlete, he displayed extraordinary talent on the basketball court, amassing a staggering 2,000 points for Bridgewater Men’s Basketball. His dedication, skill, and unwavering commitment to excellence were evident in every game he played, earning him a place among the college’s all-time greats.

Beyond his exploits as an athlete, Dr. Reedy continued his journey with Bridgewater College as a dedicated professor, where he shared his knowledge and passion with countless students. His impact on the academic community was profound, and his commitment to nurturing young minds left a lasting legacy.

In addition to his role as a professor, Dr. Reedy served as an athletic director, further demonstrating his dedication to Bridgewater Athletics. His leadership and vision contributed to the growth and success of the athletic programs, ensuring that Bridgewater College continued to thrive as a hub of sportsmanship and achievement.

Furthermore, Dr. Reedy assumed the role of head coach for Bridgewater Baseball, where he mentored and inspired generations of athletes to reach their full potential on and off the field. His coaching prowess and mentorship transcended the game itself, instilling values of teamwork, perseverance, and dedication in his players.

As we remember Dr. James “Jim” Reedy, we honor his legacy of excellence in Bridgewater Athletics. His contributions as a student-athlete, professor, athletic director, and coach have left an indelible mark on our community. Our thoughts and condolences go out to his family, friends, and all those whose lives were touched by his remarkable presence. Dr. Reedy will forever be cherished and remembered for his profound impact on Bridgewater College and its athletic tradition.
