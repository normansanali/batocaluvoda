Princess Beatrice Attends Prince Napolon's Royal Wedding

  • Princess Beatrice and her fiancé, Edoardo Mapelli Mozzi, attended Jean-Christophe, Prince Napoléon's wedding to Countess Olympia von und zu Arco-Zinnerberg this weekend.
  • Jean-Christophe is a descendent of his namesake Napoleon Bonaparte, while Countess Olympia is the great-great-great niece of Napoleon's second wife.
  • Beatrice announced her engagement to property tycoon Mozzi in September 2019.

Princess Beatrice and her soon-to-be husband, Edoardo Mapelli Mozzi, stepped out in Paris, France this weekend for an important wedding.

The princess and her beau were photographed at the nuptials of Jean-Christophe, Prince Napoléon, a descendant of Napoleon Bonaparte. Prince Napoléon tied the knot with Countess Olympia von und zu Arco-Zinnerberg, the great-great-great niece of Archduchess Marie-Louise of Austria, Napoleon's second wife, to whom he is only distantly related by marriage.

As usual, Beatrice and Mozzi looked smitten as they attended the wedding ceremony, which took place at Les Invalides in Paris, France. Princess Beatrice looked pretty in a floral dress by The Vampire's Wife, which is currently on sale for $900.
