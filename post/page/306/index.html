Seaside Hollow - Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokmon encyclopedia

published on August 16, 2024
From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia. The Seaside Hollow (Japanese: 海辺の小穴 Seaside Hollow) is a small cave located in the northern region of the Cobalt Coastlands of Hisui. It is accessible through an opening on the northern cliffside of Veilstone Cape, which is to the west of the Tidal Passage. Ride Basculegion is required to reach it.
