Michael Owen also known as Michael James Owen is an English former football player, sports pundit, commentator, analyst and racehorse breeder. As of 2023, Michael Owen’s net worth is $68 million. Owen is regarded as one of the all-time best English strikers who has represented his country’s national team for a long period. Besides that, he graced his presence in the striker position for A-list teams like Real Madrid, Liverpool, Newcastle Utd, Stoke City, and Manchester United.

Owen who got inspired and influenced by his father Terry Owen started his hands on this game at the age of 7. While he was studying at the Deeside Area Primary School, his journey in this football career began. His play style includes an aggressive approach and as a striker, he cut like a sharp knife through the opponent’s defense. As he signed a deal with Liverpool, on his debut he became the youngest player to score a goal in the Premier League.
