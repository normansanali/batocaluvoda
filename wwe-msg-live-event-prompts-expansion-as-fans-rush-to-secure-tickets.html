WWE MSG Live Event Prompts Expansion as Fans Rush to Secure Tickets

It is always a big deal whenver WWE makes its way to Madison Square Garden for their shows. WWE will begin their Holiday tour tonight at the World’s Most Famous Arena and now it appears the arena has opened up more sections as tickets continue to sell.

The upcoming MSG show is set to unfold on Tuesday, December 26, 2023. Anticipated to feature a stacked lineup, the match card for the event promises to showcase premium live event-level bouts.

CM Punk is scheduled to enter the ring against Dominik Mysterio as part of WWE’s Live Holiday Tour on Tuesday, December 26th at Madison Square Garden – no doubt a massive draw for fans.

According to WrestleTix on Twitter, it was revealed that 6 new sections have been added to Madison Square Garden as the event has sold 15,569 tickets out of a total capacity of 16,172 right now.

”WWE Live Holiday Tour
Tue • Dec 26 • 7:30 PM
Madison Square Garden, New York, NY

Available Tickets => 603
Current Setup => 16,172
Tickets Distributed => 15,569

6 new sections have been added as highlighted below. They are all $75 per ticket and designated as “Full view of ring & no view of entrance ramp.” +252 since the last update (1 day ago).

Here are the most recent Pollstar entries I could find for past Holiday MSG shows:

12/26/2019: 10,795 paid ($635,882)
12/26/2018: 13,032 paid ($769,029)
12/28/2008: 18,224 paid ($764,175)‘

The show will also feature a Bull Rope match, pitting Cody Rhodes against Shinsuke Nakamura in this stipulated contest. Seth Rollins is also set to defend his World Heavyweight Championship against Drew McIntyre in a Grudge Match on the WWE Day 1 edition of RAW. We’ll have to wait and see how the live event will turn out.
