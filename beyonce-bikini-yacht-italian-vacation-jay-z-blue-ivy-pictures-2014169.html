Beyonce Poses in Bikini on Yacht During Italian Vacation: Pictures

Now that her On the Run Tour has officially wrapped, Beyonce finally got around to uploading those vacation photos. The Grammy winner, 33, took a yacht vacation with husband, Jay Z, and daughter, Blue Ivy, 2, to Portofino, Italy, prior to the couple’s final Paris tour dates earlier this month. 

The sexy and sweet photos, which are featured on her personal website, show off the pop diva’s birthday celebrations — from her “Bey Hive” crown to a sultry personal bikini photo shoot. 

Posing on the steps of the yacht, Bey, clad in a snakeskin bikini, fearlessly showed off her entire body, which she adorned with temporary gold tattoos on her stomach, back, arms, and legs. 

She also shared a picturesque shot wearing a Camilla kaftan with her husband of six years, hugging one another on the beach.
