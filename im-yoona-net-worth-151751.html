About

Also known as simply Yoona, she is a singer, Dancer, and Actress who began performing with the girl group Girls' Generation in 2007. She had her breakout acting role on the 2008 South Korean TV series You Are My Destiny. She made her Chinese TV debut on the series God of War, Zhao Yun in 2016. 

Before Fame

She started modeling at age fourteen before beginning her singing career at age seventeen. She has attended Dongguk University for theatre and music.

Trivia

She won a KBS Drama Award for her role on the TV series Prime Minister and I in 2013. 

Family Life

She was born in Seoul, South Korea and raised with an older sister. She dated singer Lee Seung-gi for nearly two years.

Associated With

In Girls' Generation, she is joined by Taeyeon Kim, Tiffany, Yuri, Sunny, Seohyun, Hyoyeon, Sooyoung and Jessica.
