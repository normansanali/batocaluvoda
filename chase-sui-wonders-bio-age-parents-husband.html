Chase Sui Wonders Bio, Age, Parents, Husband, Children, Net Worth – Chase Charmayne Sui Wonders is a young American actress born on May 21, 1996, who has gained recognition for her performances in both film and television.

Chase Sui Wonders Biography

She began her career as a filmmaker, co-directing and writing the screenplay for the 2009 movie A Trivial Exclusion, where she also starred in the leading role. In 2015, she wrote and directed the feature film Last Migration.

Wonders has also acted in several other movies, including the 2019 psychological horror film Daniel Isn’t Real where she played Makayla, and the 2020 comedy-drama film On the Rocks, directed by Sofia Coppola, where she had a small role.

In 2021, Wonders landed a major role in the HBO Max series Generation, playing the character of Riley, a strong and confident high school student. She also wrote and directed a short film called Wake, starring herself and Charles Melton, in collaboration with Vogue China magazine in the same year.

In May 2021, Wonders joined the cast of the satirical slasher film Bodies, Bodies, Bodies, directed by Halina Reijn, and in October of the same year, she was cast in the Neil LaBute-directed Out of the Blue. In January 2022, it was announced that she would star as Samantha, the lead character of Apple TV+’s City on Fire, a series based on a book of the same name.

Aside from her acting career, Wonders also appeared as a model in a fashion campaign for a collaboration between her aunt, fashion designer Anna Sui, and Batsheva Hay in 2021.
