Erinn Hayes Biography and Wiki

Erinn Hayes is a comedian and actress from America best known for playing Dr. Lola Spratt on the sitcom Children’s Hospital, a role she later reprised on the Medical Police spin-off. She was nominated for the 2016 Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Actress in a Short Form Comedy or Drama Series for her performance.
