John Deere 35G Compact Excavator Features

Engine

The EPA Technology FT4/EU Stage IV for our excavators is straightforward, fuel-efficient, completely integrated, and completely supplied.

Auto-idle automatically slows down engine speed when the hydraulics aren’t being used. Auto-shutdown helps to preserve every bit of gas.

Tracks

Choose the tracks that work ideal for your do your work. Rubber tracks are suitable for virtually all surfaces, including asphalted surfaces. Steel tracks, as well as steel tracks that have pad-like rubber, are readily available. Flexible rubber grousers provide the flexibility of work anywhere with steel-track serviceability.

Ease of Control

Change from backhoe-style controls to excavator-style controls on the one hand. Control pattern selector valves are conveniently situated in a drawer below the seat. There is no need for operator activation to use high-speed travel.

The track speeds automatically decrease to a minimum whenever the motors of the travel system encounter an increased burden. The console is equipped with a high-speed lock.

Compact Design

The zero-tail-swing design makes this small more manoeuvrable and efficient even in tight areas.

Visibility

The new cab’s taller, wider front glass and single-hinge door give unparalleled visibility.

Serviceability

A hinged door gives wide-open access to the side-by-side oil radiator and cooler for simpler cleansing of the central area. The operator station is tilted forward 50 degrees, facilitating access to the motor swing, the hydraulic control valve, a motor starter for the engine and the alternator.
