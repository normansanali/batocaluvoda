Logan Paul brushes teeth with pet parrot Sir perched on his head ahead of Floyd Mayweather fight

LOGAN PAUL shared a photo of himself brushing his teeth onto social media – but it was the parrot on his head that caught the attention.

The YouTuber, who is fighting Floyd Mayweather on June 6, bought ‘Sir’ in April 2020.

And clearly the two are close, with the parrot seeming to like Logan’s blond locks.

The social media sensation made the shocking revelation that his previous parrot, Maverick, was EATEN by his dog.

Paul posted on Instagram that his Mastiff ‘mistook him for a small rubber chicken’.

He added: “This little bird inspired so much of what I do, he was my best friend

“And as bizarre as it sounds, this little parrot’s legacy will live on FAR beyond his life.”

“But for all the Mavericks out there who continue to see life through the lens of possibility, who continue to persevere and embrace their differences, I have never-ending love and optimism for you.

“Maverick will always be with us in spirit, and he will forever hold the largest piece of my heart. Stay strong & I will too.”

The two had been together for seven years.
