Both Matt LeBlanc and Jennifer Aniston revealed playing a guest-starring role on Friends came with a lot of pressure. In fact, LeBlanc mentioned that most of the time, guest-stars weren't up to the task, and were saved by the show's brilliant writing.

Given the fame and closeness of the cast, appearing on Friends was a difficulty for even the most established stars. We'll reveal with stars struggled on the show, while also detailing Anna Faris' time with the cast.

The actress detailed her first day on set and as expected, the environment was a stressful one, especially given that the show was coming to an end. We're going to reveal why Faris felt terrified, and how she reacted to the main cast. In addition, we'll reveal where Faris thinks her character of Erica would be today. Given her guess, it seems very accurate and one fans will definitely agree with.

Even The Recurring Guest-Stars Like Tom Selleck Were Terrified To Be On Friends

Appearing on Friends was not an easy task, even for the established actors. Playing a role in front of a live audience is one extreme for actors, and being alongside a tight cast like the one on Friends was also a different kind of beast for guest-stars. The likes of Jennifer Coolidge even tried to give their own creative output, but realized it was not the type of environment to do so.

Among the memorable guests along the way included established actor Tom Selleck. He received a strong reaction during his debut, so much so that the cheering needed to be edited behind the scenes. Despite the anticipation and welcoming, Selleck admitted the role was quite an adjustment, and praised Courteney Cox for making him feel at ease despite the nerves.

“I was scared to death,” Selleck revealed. “I had done ‘Taxi’ a long time before, but I hadn’t done a sitcom.”

“Courteney’s a big help,” he said. “That group is just an incredible group of friends. They obviously became friends in life as well as on the show, and it shows. It was a wonderful place to work, and we ended up doing about nine (episodes).”

Tom was far from the only actor who felt the nerves, despite such a polished resume. Anna Faris had experience in comedy, but she had never worked on anything like Friends.

Anna Faris Felt Terrified During Her Stint On Friends Given How Different The Show Was Compared To Anything Else She Had Done

Speaking alongside Media Village, Anna Faris recalled her first day on the set of Friends. She entered the mix near the end, and this might've added to her nerves.

She revealed, “I was really intimidated,” Faris revealed about her first day on the sitcom. “It was also a big thrill to be a part of something so iconic, but mostly I was really terrified. It was a whole new format from anything I’d done and they were all so good at it, they’d been doing it for years. So it was very intimidating and basically terrifying for me.”

Faris also admitted to being caught in emotional moments with the cast - though she felt as though she should not have been there, similar to how Paul Rudd felt with the main cast.

“It was funny because they were all in a very emotional, very sentimental place,” she said. “There would be a lot of group hugs, which sometimes I was in and sometimes not. But I always felt like I shouldn't be in them because I hadn't earned it. It really felt like a special time.”

Playing the role of Erica, Faris was praised for her involvement on the series. She also guessed where her character would be today, years after the adoption took place.

Anna Faris Predicted Where Her Erica Character On Friends Would Be Today

During the same interview, Faris joked that she hardly sees her episode play on television, despite the fact that Friends continues to be a constant. According to Faris, this might be due to the fact that it takes place near the end of the series, episodes the fans want no part of.

As for Erica's future, well Anna Faris has clearly thought this one through.

“She'd be in Missouri and is maybe an assistant manager at Walmart,” Faris said with a laugh.

“She may have gained a few pounds and maybe had another baby. I've played a lot of, for lack of a better word ‘stupid’ characters, and there is a weird anal sex joke in there that she doesn't really understand about how people get pregnant. I wish the best for Erica and I hope she's well and happy.”

Other fans were hoping for something romantic with Joey, but it was not meant to be.
