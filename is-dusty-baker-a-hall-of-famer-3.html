Is Dusty Baker a Hall of Famer?

Dusty Baker is an American baseball manager known in real life as Johnnie B. Baker. Dusty Baker currently serves as the manager for Houston Astros in Major League Baseball.

Prior to joining the Houston Astros, Dusty Baker managed the San Francisco Giants, Chicago Cubs, Cincinnati Reds, and the Washington Nationals.

Before taking up a manager role, Dusty Baker played baseball and was an outfielder for the Atlanta Braves and Los Angeles Dodgers.
