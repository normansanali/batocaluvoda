Career and Professional Life

Angela Simmons is an American reality star, social media personality, shoe designer, and entrepreneur. Likewise, the young entrepreneur first gained unprecedented exposure after she started to appear on her family’s reality television series ‘Run’s House’. The series aired in 2005. Similarly, In the year 2006, she became the editor of the mini-magazine ‘Angela’s Rundown’. The magazine was widely distributed along with the ‘Word Up!’ magazine.

The rundown was a fashion magazine for teenagers. The magazine featured articles on topics including style, music, and more. Similarly, It also included detailed interviews of “ugly duckling” celebrities. The shoe designer continued with the magazine till ‘Word Up!’ stopped publishing in April 2012. Likewise, Back in the year 2007, Angela, who had studied fashion in college, launched a footwear line. The company was named ‘Pastry Footwear’. She established the company along with her older sister Vanessa.

The young entrepreneur’s business ventures eventually became her priorities. Angela then returned to reality shows in 2009 to appear alongside her older sister in ‘Daddy’s Girls’. Later on, the entrepreneur launched her own fashion accessories line and opened up eCommerce websites. The reality star also continued to appear on several reality shows and television series. Some of the reality series she appeared in are ‘Fashion News Live’ (2008-2014), ‘Growing Up Hip Hop’ (2016), and ‘The Real’ (2016). Moreover, she later became the executive producer on the reality show, Growing Up Hip-hop in the year 2016.
