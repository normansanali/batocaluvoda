Ric Flair DVD Update Inside, Was Bret Hart At FCW Planned?


The contents of the upcoming Nature Boy Ric Flair: The Definitive Collection three disc DVD set that is scheduled to be released on July 8 have been disclosed. Unlike the first DVD set, this one will feature a long documentary on Flair highlighting his entire career through his farewell on Raw. The chapter on his time in WCW during the late 90s is entitled “Troubles in WCW.” The next chapter featuring his return to WWE in 2001 is entitled “Success in WWE.” The DVD set will feature his farewell on Raw, including footage that never aired on television.

Bret Hart speaking with Florida Championship Wrestling talent two weeks ago was not planned. Bret was in Florida hanging out with a friend from TNA when Natalya Neidhart asked him if he could come down to the FCW center to speak in front of the wrestlers, reports the Wrestling Observer Newsletter. It was an impromptu thing and Bret reportedly enjoyed it.

Devin Cutting sent in the following… Kid Kash and Cassidy Riley will be appearing at the Showtime All-Star Wrestling event on May 16th, 2008 in Millersville, Tennessee. Riley will also be at the SAW event in Columbia, Tennessee on May 17th, 2008.
