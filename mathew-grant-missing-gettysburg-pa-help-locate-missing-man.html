Mathew Grant Missing – One of the individuals who had been missing for more than three weeks and who was a student at Rochester Institute of Technology has been found and is currently in a safe location. Examining the individual who was the centre of the investigation was the primary emphasis of the examination.

The Monroe County Sheriff’s Office has issued a press statement that has been disseminated today. The statement states that Matthew Grant, who is 22 years old, has been identified and has been reunited with his family. This information was available in the statement that was delivered. The announcement was made on Wednesday, which was the next day.

To the best of our knowledge, neither Grant’s whereabouts nor the circumstances surrounding his discovery were disclosed to the general public under any circumstances. Neither do we have any information regarding the circumstances that led to his finding. “Thank you to all of the deputies and investigators who worked so tenaciously coordinating with multiple agencies,

following up leads, using technology, and working with the family to locate Matthew,” according to a statement posted by the Sheriff’s Office. “We appreciate our partnership with the media who helped spread awareness and our efforts to reunite him with his family.”
